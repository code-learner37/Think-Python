1. 42 mins 42 sec
    There are 42 * 60 + 42 = 2562 seconds.
2. 1 miles = 1.61km
    10 km = 10 / 1.61 = 6.211 miles
3. 10 km in 42 mins 42 sec, so the average speed is 6.211 / (42 + 42 /     60) = 0.145 miles per min. Average speed in miles per hour is 6.211     / (42 / 60 + 42 / 3600) = 8.727
